You can find out that the much colder the country is, the higher suicide rate might be. For example, it is always hot in Thailand so the suicide rate is lower there while the higher suicide rate in Russia might be affected by the cold weather. Although there are lots of reports indicating that the temperature might not have a strong direct relationship with suicides, the colder weather still may be a reason to cause people to commit suicide.
